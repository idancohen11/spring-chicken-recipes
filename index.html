Spring Chicken

Eight timeless recipes celebrating tender, young poultry

Spring chicken, also known as poussin, represents the pinnacle of tenderness and delicate flavor. These young birds, typically 4-6 weeks old, have been celebrated in kitchens across the world for centuries. Here, we present a curated collection of recipes that honor both tradition and innovation.

01

Herb-Roasted Spring Chicken with Lemon & Thyme

A classic preparation that lets the natural flavors shine. Fresh herbs, garlic, and bright lemon create an aromatic crust while keeping the meat incredibly moist.

⏱ 1h 15min 👥 2-3 servings 🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken (about 500g)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh thyme (6-8 sprigs)
  • Fresh rosemary (2 sprigs)
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Pat chicken dry and season cavity with salt and pepper. Stuff with lemon halves and half the herbs.
  3. Mix butter with minced garlic and remaining chopped herbs. Rub under and over the skin.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Roast for 45-55 minutes until golden and juices run clear (internal temp 74°C/165°F).
  6. Rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with pan juices.
02

Honey-Glazed Spring Chicken with Mustard

Sweet meets savory in this caramelized masterpiece. The honey glaze creates a beautiful lacquered finish with a subtle tang from Dijon mustard.

⏱ 1h 👥 2 servings 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken (about 500g)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Whisk together honey, mustard, soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, and paprika.
  3. Pat chicken dry and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Brush half the glaze over the chicken, reserving the rest.
  5. Roast for 40 minutes, basting with remaining glaze every 15 minutes.
  6. Increase heat to 220°C (425°F) for final 5 minutes to caramelize.
  7. Rest for 5 minutes before serving with the pan glaze.
03

Mediterranean Spring Chicken with Olives & Tomatoes

Transport yourself to the sun-drenched coasts with this vibrant one-pan wonder. Cherry tomatoes burst into a rich sauce while olives add briny depth.

⏱ 1h 10min 👥 2-3 servings 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken, spatchcocked
  • 300g cherry tomatoes
  • 100g Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 4 cloves garlic, whole
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a large roasting pan, combine tomatoes, olives, onion, and garlic.
  3. Place spatchcocked chicken on top, skin-side up. Drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Season generously with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Roast for 50-60 minutes until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes, then garnish with fresh basil.
  7. Serve with crusty bread to soak up the delicious pan juices.
04

Asian-Inspired Spring Chicken with Ginger & Soy

Fragrant ginger, aromatic garlic, and umami-rich soy sauce create a marinade that penetrates every fiber. Finished with sesame oil for nutty depth.

⏱ 1h 30min 👥 2 servings 🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken (about 500g)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice wine or sherry
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 3cm ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp five-spice powder (optional)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, rice wine, honey, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, and five-spice.
  2. Marinate chicken for at least 1 hour (or overnight in fridge).
  3. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  4. Place chicken in roasting pan and pour marinade over.
  5. Roast for 50-60 minutes, basting every 20 minutes.
  6. Increase heat to 220°C (425°F) for final 10 minutes to crisp skin.
  7. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
05

Garlic Butter Spring Chicken with Herbs

Indulgent and aromatic, this butter-basted bird is a celebration of simplicity. The garlic-herb butter creates a golden, crispy skin while keeping the meat succulent.

⏱ 1h 20min 👥 2-3 servings 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken (about 500g)
  • 100g butter, softened
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh tarragon, chopped
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh thyme sprigs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Mix softened butter with garlic, parsley, tarragon, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
  3. Carefully loosen chicken skin and spread half the butter underneath.
  4. Rub remaining butter all over the outside. Stuff cavity with thyme and lemon halves.
  5. Roast for 50-60 minutes, basting with melted butter every 15 minutes.
  6. Rest for 10 minutes. Drizzle with lemon juice before serving.
06

Moroccan-Spiced Spring Chicken with Apricots

An exotic journey to North Africa with warming spices, sweet dried apricots, and toasted almonds. The chicken becomes wonderfully aromatic and tender.

⏱ 1h 15min 👥 2-3 servings 🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken, spatchcocked
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 100g dried apricots, halved
  • 50g slivered almonds, toasted
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Mix spices with salt, pepper, and olive oil to form a paste.
  3. Rub spice paste all over the spatchcocked chicken.
  4. Place in roasting pan and scatter apricots around.
  5. Roast for 45-50 minutes. Drizzle honey over chicken for last 10 minutes.
  6. Rest for 5 minutes, then garnish with toasted almonds and cilantro.
07

Spring Chicken in White Wine & Cream Sauce

A French-inspired classic that's pure elegance. The chicken braises in white wine and cream, creating a luscious sauce perfect for spooning over mashed potatoes.

⏱ 1h 30min 👥 2-3 servings 🔥 Advanced

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken, jointed
  • 200ml dry white wine
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh tarragon
  • Salt and white pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken pieces with salt and white pepper.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large skillet. Brown chicken on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté shallots and garlic until soft. Add mushrooms and cook until golden.
  4. Pour in white wine and scrape up browned bits. Reduce by half.
  5. Return chicken to pan, add cream and tarragon. Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Uncover and reduce sauce until it coats the back of a spoon.
  7. Serve immediately with crusty bread or creamy mashed potatoes.
08

Citrus-Glazed Spring Chicken with Orange & Lime

Bright, zesty, and utterly refreshing. The combination of orange and lime creates a tangy glaze that caramelizes beautifully, complementing the tender meat.

⏱ 1h 10min 👥 2 servings 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 spring chicken (about 500g)
  • 2 oranges (zest and juice)
  • 2 limes (zest and juice)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds, crushed
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Mix orange and lime zest and juice with honey, olive oil, garlic, and coriander.
  3. Reserve half the marinade. Coat chicken with the other half and marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Place chicken in roasting pan. Roast for 50-60 minutes, basting with reserved marinade.
  5. Increase heat to 220°C (425°F) for final 5 minutes to caramelize the glaze.
  6. Rest for 5 minutes, garnish with cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
